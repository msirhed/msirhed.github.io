Education

Trusst’s educational wing is modelled after the nation’s leading educational research facilities, matching comprehensive, focused learning with real-world applications. Under the guidance of our experienced instructors, our members look at our clients’ requests as learning and research opportunities.

Memberships

Memberships are paid if you come for sessions. Once you purchase your card, you come and you will not need to pay on the spot. If you take classes like photography or design, you may need to pay an extra fee for needed supplies.

Silver

  • 1-2 day classes a week recommended

Gold

  • 2-3 days a week recommended

Platinum

  • $300
  • 3-5 days recommended
